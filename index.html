4 GoHighLevel Snapshot — Automated Client Onboarding

📸 What Is a GHL Snapshot?

A Snapshot is a pre-built template package in GoHighLevel that contains all workflows, pipelines, automations, AI agent configurations, email/SMS templates, forms, and settings. When a new EPIC client signs, Shane deploys the snapshot to instantly create a fully configured sub-account — eliminating hours of manual setup per client. This is EPIC's core scalability engine.

1 Snapshot Contains

Pre-built pipelines (Lead → Booked → Won), AI voice agent base config, missed-call text-back workflow, appointment booking automation, follow-up sequences (email + SMS), welcome campaign, review request workflow, estimate follow-up, reporting dashboard template, EPIC branding (not True Farm Welfare).

2 Deployment Trigger

After MSA signed + Stripe payment confirmed → Shane navigates to Agency View → Sub-Accounts → Create New → Apply EPIC master snapshot. This auto-populates the entire sub-account with all templates, workflows, and base AI agent config in under 2 minutes.

3 Post-Snapshot Customization

After snapshot deployment, Shane customizes: business name & contact info, service descriptions & pricing ranges, AI agent greeting & intent routing, Twilio phone number assignment, niche-specific addendum (Plumbing/HVAC/Roofing KB), booking calendar link, and Stripe Connect for client payments.

4 Niche Addendums (Knowledge Base)

Each niche has a pre-built KB addendum: Plumbing (emergency calls, leak repair, water heater, drain cleaning), Roofing (storm damage, insurance intake, inspection booking), HVAC (AC repair, heating emergency, maintenance plans). Loaded into the AI agent and chatbot per client's niche.

5 Automation Target

Future state: trigger snapshot deployment via API/webhook when Stripe payment is confirmed — fully automated sub-account creation without manual intervention. AI agent (from Agent Blueprint) handles the GHL API calls to create sub-account, apply snapshot, and configure base settings.

6 QA & Go-Live

After customization: run 5+ test calls across all intents, verify booking flow, check missed-call text-back, validate SMS/email templates, confirm CRM integration. Client previews and approves. Then flip the number live and monitor first 48 hours.

1. DEFINITIONS

  • 1.1 "Services" — AI voice agents, chatbots, CRM integrations, automation workflows, and related implementation services.
  • 1.2 "Platform" — GoHighLevel ("GHL") and connected third-party systems.
  • 1.3 "Third-Party Software" — CRM systems: ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, Leap, AccuLynx, JobNimbus, etc.
  • 1.4 "AI Agent" — automated voice and/or chat systems deployed on behalf of Client.
  • 1.5 "Confidential Information" — all non-public business, operational, and customer information shared between parties.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

EPIC agrees to design, configure, deploy, and maintain: AI voice call answering, web/SMS chatbot systems, appointment booking workflows, missed-call text-back, reschedule/cancellation handling, estimate follow-up automation, annual nurture campaigns, CRM integration and routing logic. All Services implemented within GoHighLevel. Industry-specific features defined in applicable Addendum.

3. IMPLEMENTATION & ONBOARDING

Client provides access to CRM, phone systems, domains, scheduling calendars. Client must provide accurate service descriptions, pricing ranges, hours, and coverage areas. EPIC not responsible for delays from incomplete/inaccurate info.

4. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Maintain CRM/telephony access, ensure compliance with call recording/messaging laws (TCPA, state laws), review AI scripts before deployment, maintain updated availability, handle customer fulfillment. EPIC does not perform field services.

5. SUBSCRIPTION FEES & PAYMENT

Fees as defined in Schedule A. Billed monthly, due in advance. Late payments may result in suspension.

6. FREE TRIAL (IF APPLICABLE)

Duration defined in Schedule A. Client may cancel before expiration. Continued use after trial = acceptance of paid subscription.

7. TERM & TERMINATION

Month-to-month unless otherwise specified. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. EPIC may terminate immediately for non-payment or breach.

8. DATA OWNERSHIP

Client owns customer data. EPIC retains ownership of automation architecture, AI scripts, workflow logic, and system configurations. Upon termination, Client retains customer data but not proprietary system design.

9. THIRD-PARTY DISCLAIMER

EPIC not responsible for CRM outages, telephony failures, API limitations, or third-party pricing changes.

10. AI LIMITATIONS

AI agents may occasionally misunderstand callers. Speech recognition errors may occur. EPIC does not guarantee booking rates or revenue increases.

11. NO REVENUE GUARANTEE

EPIC provides automation systems only. Results depend on market conditions, pricing, staffing, and operational performance.

12. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Total liability capped at fees paid in previous 3 months. Not liable for lost profits, missed calls, or consequential damages.

13–16. CONFIDENTIALITY, INDEMNIFICATION, GOVERNING LAW, ENTIRE AGREEMENT

Both parties protect confidential info. Client indemnifies EPIC against claims from customer disputes, misrepresentation, or telemarketing law violations. Governed by Missouri law. Agreement + Addendums + Schedule A = entire agreement.

ADDENDUM A — PLUMBING

Emergency plumbing call handling, leak repair, drain cleaning, water heater scheduling, after-hours overflow, dispatch routing. AI captures property type, urgency, water damage indicators.

ADDENDUM B — ROOFING

Storm damage inspection booking, insurance claim intake, roof replacement scheduling, residential vs commercial qualification. AI captures roof type, storm timing, insurance status.

ADDENDUM C — HVAC

AC repair scheduling, heating emergencies, seasonal maintenance plans, service contract renewals. AI captures system type, unit age, emergency status.

SCHEDULE A — PRICING

Three tiers: Basic (AI Call Answering), Professional (Voice + Chat), Enterprise (Full Suite). Setup fee, optional trial (7 or 14 day), usage-based overage for voice minutes/SMS/AI conversations. Add-ons: additional locations, multi-number routing, custom scripts, advanced reporting, dedicated account manager, white-label. Month-to-month, 6-month, or 12-month terms. Auto-billing via credit card or ACH.

DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM (DPA)

EPIC = Processor, Client = Controller. Processes personal data (names, phones, emails, addresses, call recordings, SMS, appointment data) solely to deliver Services. EPIC implements access controls, role-based permissions, encrypted transmission. Subprocessors include GHL, telephony/SMS providers, CRM systems, cloud hosting. Data retained for Agreement duration; Client may request export upon termination. Breach notification without undue delay.
