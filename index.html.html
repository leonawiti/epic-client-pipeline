2 Stage-by-Stage Workflow

🎯 Stage 1: Prospecting & Lead Generation

Goal: Fill the top of funnel with qualified SMB leads

  • Scrape leads by niche (plumbers, HVAC, roofers, lawyers) via Appify
  • Clean, deduplicate, and enrich contact data in Google Sheets
  • Import validated leads into GoHighLevel CRM
  • Tag leads by niche, geography, and source
  • Launch voicemail drop campaigns (200/day via GHL + Twilio)
  • Run LinkedIn outreach via Make.com scenarios
  • Post to social media (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram)
  • A/B test outreach scripts weekly — rotate and measure

Tools: Appify, Google Sheets, GoHighLevel, Make.com, 11 Labs, Twilio, truereach.rep

Owner: Leon (lead gen) · Shane (GHL campaigns)

📞 Stage 2: Engagement & Qualification

Goal: Convert callbacks and inquiries into booked demos

  • AI agent (Alexis) answers inbound calls — greeting: simple "Hello"
  • Alexis qualifies: business type, pain points, timeline, budget
  • If callback from voicemail → acknowledge the VM drop first
  • Route to booking flow: capture name, email, phone, business name
  • Book free automation audit / discovery call on calendar
  • Auto-send confirmation email + SMS with calendar link
  • Tag lead as "Demo Booked" in GHL pipeline
  • All calls recorded automatically for training + quality review

Tools: GoHighLevel (AI agent), 11 Labs (Zoe voice), Google Calendar

Owner: AI Agent (Alexis) · Shane (prompt tuning)

🤝 Stage 3: Discovery & Proposal

Goal: Understand the client's workflow and present a tailored solution

  • Conduct 15-minute discovery call (Leon or Shane)
  • Use qualifying questions from GHL Knowledge Base (Article 2.2)
  • Identify automation opportunities: calls, scheduling, follow-up, billing
  • Determine average ticket value → calculate pricing via pricing calculator
  • Present service tier: Basic / Professional / Enterprise
  • Share relevant case studies or demo of AI voice agent
  • Handle objections using EPIC objection playbook
  • If interested → send MSA contract + Schedule A for review

Tools: Zoom/Meet, Pricing Calculator (to build), MSA template (Google Docs)

Owner: Leon (sales lead) · Shane (technical demo)

✍️ Stage 4: Close & Contract

Goal: Signed contract + payment + GHL sub-account created

  • Client reviews and signs MSA + Schedule A + DPA
  • Collect first payment via Stripe (monthly subscription)
  • Create dedicated GHL sub-account for client
  • Apply EPIC branding snapshot to sub-account (not True Farm Welfare)
  • Set up client's phone number + A2P registration
  • Tag in CRM: "Client — Active" + assign account manager
  • Send welcome email with onboarding timeline
  • Trigger onboarding workflow in GHL

Tools: Google Docs (contract), Stripe (billing), GoHighLevel (sub-account + snapshot)

Owner: Leon (contract) · Shane (GHL setup + snapshot)

🚀 Stage 5: Onboarding & Deployment

Goal: Fully configured AI voice agent + chatbot + automations live for client

  • Kickoff call: confirm scope, services, CRM access, business hours
  • Collect client's top 10 FAQs, service descriptions, pricing ranges
  • Build custom Knowledge Base from EPIC template (per niche addendum)
  • Configure AI voice agent: greeting, intent routing, booking flow
  • Configure website chatbot with same KB
  • Set up automation workflows: missed-call text-back, follow-ups, nurture
  • Integrate with client's existing CRM (ServiceTitan, Jobber, etc.) if applicable
  • Internal QA: test all call flows, chat flows, booking, edge cases
  • Client preview + approval of scripts before go-live
  • Go-live: flip number, activate automations, monitor first 48 hours

Tools: GoHighLevel, 11 Labs, Twilio, client CRM

Owner: Shane (GHL build) · Leon (KB + QA)

Timeline: 3–5 business days from contract signed to go-live

📊 Stage 6: Managed Service & Growth

Goal: Retain client, demonstrate ROI, and expand scope

  • Weekly campaign analytics report (contacts reached, calls booked, conversion rate)
  • Monthly performance review call with client
  • Quarterly optimization: refine prompts, update KB, tune workflows
  • Monitor AI call quality — flag and fix issues within 24 hours
  • Upsell opportunities: additional locations, advanced reporting, more automations
  • Handle renewal, rate adjustments (30-day notice per MSA)
  • Collect testimonials and case study data for marketing

Tools: GoHighLevel (reporting), Google Sheets, Stripe (billing)

Owner: Leon (account management) · Shane (technical optimization)
